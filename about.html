Who the hell is this guy?

My name is Dan, a freelance videographer, photographer and content producer based in East London.

I have worked within the industry for numerous years working alongside brands of all sizes ranging from NikeTown London, to independent brands just starting out, helping them create content to promote their products or to tell their story to introduce their brand to future clients.

I love to work closely with the brand I am working with to make sure they receive the most personable content possible ranging from product photography, event videography, documentary style and bespoke social media content.
